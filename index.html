Daniel Bernier

I am a piano technician located in Woonsocket, Rhode Island.

I tune all types of acoustic pianos: grands, uprights, consoles and spinets.

I service Rhode Island, including Woonsocket, Smithfield, Johnston, Lincoln, Scituate, Cumberland, Pawtucket, Providence, Cranston, Warwick, Bristol, and East Greenwich. I also service Massachussetts, including Bellingham, Attleboro, Foxborough, Norton, Uxbridge, Milford, and Wrentham.

About me

I am the secretary and librarian for the Rhode Island chapter of the Piano Technician's Guild.

I study at The Butler School under Maggie Jusiel, I am mentored by Amy Tiernan and Ruth Van Dine, and I do repair work at Loiselle Pianos.
